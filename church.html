About Lusaka Baptist Church

Lusaka Baptist Church is a vibrant, Spirit-filled congregation rooted in the Word of God and the fellowship of believers. Founded in 1974, we have grown into one of Lusaka's most active Baptist communities — over 1,200 members strong and still growing.

We are committed to the Great Commission: making disciples, planting churches, and reaching every corner of Zambia with the gospel of Jesus Christ. Our doors are open to all — the young, the old, the seeker, and the faithful.

Our vision: "Rooted in faith, reaching the city."
